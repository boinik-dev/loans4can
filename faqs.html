What is a personal loan?

A personal loan is an unsecured loan that has a fixed payment and term. The funds can be used for whatever your needs may be.

Am I eligible for a personal loan?

Applicants who meet the following criteria will be considered:

Be at least 18 years old.
Provide proof of citizenship, such as a Social Insurance Number (SIN), or valid proof of address
Have a checking account in your name
Provide work and home telephone numbers
Provide a valid email address

How do I apply for a personal loan?

All you have to do is apply online, it is easy, secure and will take you five minutes. A CashLoaner lending specialist will contact you within 1 business day by phone or email to discuss your loan application.

When will I receive my loan?

Once your loan has been approved, you will receive a call from one of our lending specialists to discuss your loan options and the steps required to receive your funds.

How do I make my monthly payments?

Your monthly payment will be automatically debited from your bank account.

Is there a penalty for paying out the loan early or making additional principal payments?

No, the loan is fully open and payments can be made at any time

What area does loans4can service?

We service everywhere in Canada. As long as you are a resident of Canada we will service you

What is collateral security?

Collateral is something you own. For a personal loan, automobiles or household goods may be used as security for the loan.

What if my lender asking for a deposit?

Neither Loans4Can nor its other partners will ever require you to pay a deposit for an unsecured loan. In fact, this is a common scam and scammers may at times impersonate other, legitimate firms to trick individuals into sending them money. If you are being asked to pay an “insurance deposit” through pre-paid cards or other unusual methods, please be very careful as it is very difficult for you to get your money back. if in future you get lenders like that please don’t co-operate with them .

I’ve been rejected by a bank. What do I do?

If you’ve been rejected by a bank please note that there are still MANY options available to you. We recommend that you try again with us: you’ll benefit from better rates, more products and more options. One of the great things about Loans4can is that we have relationships with many banks and other lending institutions. We also have a large array of private lenders ready to assist you. We have relationships in place to pair you with the best solution available.

I’m a recent immigrant. Can I still apply?

Yes. Even if you have no credit history we will be able to pair you with a great financial product at a competitive rate.

Do you service the self-employed?

Yes. Loans4can is a specialist and industry leader in loans and financial solutions for self-employed individuals.

Will you check my credit score after I apply?

You must provide consent before Loans4can or its affiliates can check your credit score. Depending on the application you select, consent to check credit may be requested on our website but it is not a requirement to your application.

How do I know if I’m approved?

You will be notified as soon as your application is approved. One of our partners will call you and e-mail you to inform you that your application was approved. Loans4can strives to provide its customers with the fastest approval times in the country.
How can I speed up the application process?
After you submit your application or callback request you might be asked to submit some documents. This will depend on the product you are interested in. If such a request is made you should strive to get the required documents in as soon as possible so that the agent looking after your application can proceed with the approval process as quickly as possible. You will only be asked to provide documents that are necessary or required by law to complete your file. Loans4can protects your privacy.

What happens after I apply?

Shortly after you submit your application or callback request one of our partners will be contacting you to discuss the services you are looking for and the options available to you. The agent assigned to your application is a fully trained professional ready to assist you with all of your requirements. Loans4can will not begin to work on your file before first directly speaking to you about your financial needs.
